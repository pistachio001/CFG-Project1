The History Of Our Show

Last year, we celebrated 40 years of successful shows in the village. On looking back through the files, the earliest reference is to a meeting held in December 1980 at the Derby Arms in Rainford to explore the possibility of there being an annual show.
The objective being to encourage local residents to take part through their favourite hobbies and pastimes - and to bring their best exhibits to the show for due recognition from the judges and public.
